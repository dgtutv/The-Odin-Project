Sails 'n' Hash

Description

Our signature take on a breakfast hash

Ingredients

  • Pulled Pork
  • Juliene Red Peppers
  • Juliene Red Onions
  • One Order of Smashed Fries
  • Bloody Mary Sauce
  • Two Poached Eggs
  • One Slice of Garlic Toast
  • Parmesean
  • Chives

Steps

  1. Oil a pan and place onto the heat
  2. Place the slice of garlic toast onto the flat top
  3. Sear the peppers and onions
  4. Throw in the ham, cook until ham is seared
  5. Flip the garlic toast
  6. Throw in the smashed fries
  7. Throw in some bloody mary sauce
  8. Build recipe as shown above
