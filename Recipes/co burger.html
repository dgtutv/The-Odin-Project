THE HARD BEAN BRUNCH COMPANY

Hard Bean Co Burger

Description

Delicious brunch take on a classic burger

Ingredients

  • Burger Patty
  • Brioche Bun
  • Burger Sauce
  • Cheddar Slice
  • Two Strips of Bacon
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Shoestring Fries
  • One Egg
  • Shredded Lettuce
  • Sliced Tomatoes
  • Sliced Pickles

Steps

  1. Season both sides of the burger patty
  2. Place the burger patty on a 350F flat top (griddle)
  3. Once the burger patty starts bleeding:
    1. Flip the patty
    2. Place the brioche bun on the flat top
    3. Drop the shoestring fries into a 350F fryer, lift after 3 minutes
    4. Place cheddar on the patty
    5. Place the strips of bacon onto the flat top
    6. Oil a spot on the flat top and crack the egg onto the oil
  4. When first possible, flip the egg, then cook it for 30 seconds and place on burger
  5. When cooked, place the bacon on the burger in a cross shape
  6. When the bun is soft on top, move over to cutting board
  7. Apply burger sauce to the top and the bottom buns
  8. Place shredded lettuce on the top bun
  9. Place a tomato slice on top of the lettuce
  10. Place 4 slices of pickles onto the bottom bun
  11. Place the cooked burger patty with all of it's toppings on top of the pickle slices
  12. Assemble the plate as above
