Svale's Boaty Brunch!

Boat Broccoli Bowll

Description

Lemony salmon taste combined with fresh simmered and seared veggies

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Lemon Basil Crust
  • Quinuoa
  • Broccolini
  • Blanched Cauliflower
  • Blanched Brusselsprouts
  • Shoestring Carrots
  • Lemon Basil Marinade

Steps

  1. Sear the salmon on one side
  2. Flip the salmon
  3. Apply lemon basil crust to the seared side of the salmon
  4. Throw the salmon in a 450F oven
  5. Sear the brusselsprouts, cauliflower, and carrots
  6. Throw in the quinuoa and some lemon basil marinade
  7. Assemble all ingredients as shown above
